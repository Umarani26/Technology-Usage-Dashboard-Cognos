# Demographics Analysis

This dashboard shows the demographic distribution of survey respondents.

It includes:
- Age distribution of developers
- Country-wise participation
- Education levels
- Age vs education comparison

This helps understand the background of respondents and their learning patterns.

## Key Trends: 
Respondents are mainly 25–44 years old, with Bachelor’s degree holders forming the largest group. Most responses come from the United States, 
indicating a highly educated and US-skewed sample.

## Finding: 
The survey is dominated by educated respondents aged 25–44, with the US contributing the most responses.

## Implication: 
Results are most representative of young-to-mid-career professionals and may be less applicable to older, younger, or less-educated populations.
